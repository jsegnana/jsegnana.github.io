Welcome! My name is Juan Segnana and I am a third-year PhD Candidate
in Economics at [Tilburg University](https://www.tilburguniversity.edu/).

My research interests lie at the crossroads of **developmental**, **migration**,
and **political economy**. With a focus on developing countries, my work
utilizes administrative, survey, and geo-spatial data to answer questions on:

- The persistent effects of migration on interethnic social cohesion and local attitudes
- Assistance and welfare
- Governance under statelessness
- Nutrition and child development

I am supervised by [prof. dr. Patricio Dalton](https://patriciodalton.wordpress.com/)
and prof. dr. Daan van Soest.
