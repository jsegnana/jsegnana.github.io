Welcome! My name is Juan Segnana and I am a third-year PhD Candidate in Economics at Tilburg University. My research interests lie at the crossroads of development economics, migration, and political economy.  With a focus on developing countries, my work utilizes administrative, survey, and geo-spatial data to answer questions on:

- The persistent effects of migration on local attitudes
- Perception-reality gaps
- Assistance and welfare
- Trade reforms and refugee assimilation in export-oriented industries
- Nutrition and child development

I am supervised by [Professor Patricio Dalton](https://patriciodalton.wordpress.com/)
and [Professor Daan van Soest](https://sites.google.com/site/daanvansoest/).
