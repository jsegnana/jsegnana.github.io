I am a third-year PhD Candidate
in Economics at [Tilburg University](https://www.tilburguniversity.edu/).

My research lies at the crossroads of development economics, migration, and political economy.  With a focus on developing countries, my work utilizes administrative, survey, and geo-spatial data to study how institutional context shapes the assimilation of migrants and its effects on welfare.

I am supervised by [Professor Patricio Dalton](https://patriciodalton.wordpress.com/)
and [Professor Daan van Soest](https://sites.google.com/site/daanvansoest/).
